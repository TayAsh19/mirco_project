VIDEOS FOR A STADIUM

April 6, 2017

Location: Commonwealth Stadium Time: 7:00-9:00PM

WHAT IS VIDEOS FOR A STADIUM?

This unique ‘art meets sports’ event brings together videos that explore competition, skill, endurance, teamwork, and bodily movement. The national anthem, the green grass of various fields, and sports language are also examined. These will be presented on the stadium’s high resolution screen and sound system, and watched from seats in the end zone. Concession food and drinks that are normally available during games will be offered for purchase before and after the screening.
